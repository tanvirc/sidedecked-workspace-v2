# Story 10.2.1: Automated Escrow for High-Value Trades

**Epic**: [epic-10-payment-processing.md](../epics/epic-10-payment-processing.md)
**Status**: not_started
**Domain**: Commerce (backend/)

## User Story

_As a buyer of expensive cards, I want escrow protection so that I can safely purchase high-value items with confidence._

## Acceptance Criteria

- (NOT BUILT) Automatic escrow activation for transactions above configurable dollar thresholds
- (NOT BUILT) Secure fund holding with buyer protection until item delivery and acceptance
- (NOT BUILT) Multi-step verification process including item authentication and condition verification
- (NOT BUILT) Time-bound escrow periods with automatic release conditions and extension options
- (NOT BUILT) Professional grading service integration for high-value card authentication
- (NOT BUILT) Dispute resolution process with evidence collection and mediation services
- (NOT BUILT) Insurance integration for high-value transactions with coverage options
- (NOT BUILT) Seller protection against fraudulent disputes with evidence-based resolution
- (NOT BUILT) Integration with shipping insurance and signature confirmation requirements
- (NOT BUILT) Real-time status tracking for all parties throughout the escrow process

## Implementation Notes

Automated escrow activates above configurable thresholds and holds funds securely until item delivery and buyer acceptance. Multi-step verification, professional grading service integration, and dispute resolution with evidence collection are all required. Insurance integration and shipping confirmation tie the escrow release to verified delivery.

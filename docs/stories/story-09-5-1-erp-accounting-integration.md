# Story 9.5.1: ERP & Accounting Integration

**Epic**: [epic-09-inventory-management.md](../epics/epic-09-inventory-management.md)
**Status**: not_started
**Domain**: Both

## User Story

_As a business owner, I want full ERP integration so that inventory management integrates seamlessly with my financial and business systems._

## Acceptance Criteria

- (NOT BUILT) Complete integration with QuickBooks, Xero, NetSuite, and other major accounting systems
- (NOT BUILT) Automated journal entries for inventory purchases, sales, and adjustments
- (NOT BUILT) Cost of goods sold calculation with automatic posting to accounting system
- (NOT BUILT) Tax reporting integration with proper inventory valuation and compliance
- (NOT BUILT) Financial reporting with inventory impact on cash flow and profitability
- (NOT BUILT) Budget integration with inventory purchasing aligned to financial planning
- (NOT BUILT) Multi-entity support for businesses with multiple legal entities or locations
- (NOT BUILT) Currency conversion and international accounting standard compliance
- (NOT BUILT) Audit trail integration with accounting system for compliance and verification
- (NOT BUILT) Custom integration support for specialized business systems and requirements

## Implementation Notes

ERP and accounting integration automates journal entries for all inventory movements with COGS calculation and automatic posting. QuickBooks, Xero, and NetSuite are primary targets. Multi-entity support, currency conversion, and audit trail synchronization enable comprehensive financial reporting across all inventory activities.

# Story 10.1.3: Commission & Fee Management

**Epic**: [epic-10-payment-processing.md](../epics/epic-10-payment-processing.md)
**Status**: in_progress
**Domain**: Commerce (backend/)

## User Story

_As a marketplace, I want flexible commission and fee management so that I can optimize revenue while remaining competitive for sellers._

## Acceptance Criteria

- (IMPLEMENTED) Configurable commission rates by seller tier, product category, and transaction volume
- (IMPLEMENTED) Transparent fee calculation and display for sellers before transaction completion
- (IMPLEMENTED) Support for flat fees, percentage-based commissions, and hybrid fee structures
- (IMPLEMENTED) Volume-based commission tiers with automatic application based on seller performance
- (IN PROGRESS) Promotional fee structures for new sellers, featured products, or special events (PARTIAL)
- (IMPLEMENTED) Integration with seller agreement management and automatic fee updates
- (IMPLEMENTED) Real-time fee calculation during checkout process with clear buyer and seller visibility
- (IN PROGRESS) Commission adjustment capabilities for special circumstances or dispute resolutions (PARTIAL)
- (IMPLEMENTED) Comprehensive reporting on fee collection and revenue optimization analytics

## Implementation Notes

Configurable commission rates support seller tier, category, and volume-based structures with transparent checkout display. Flat, percentage, and hybrid fee structures are implemented with automatic tier application. Promotional fee structures for events and commission adjustments for dispute resolution are partially built and need completion.

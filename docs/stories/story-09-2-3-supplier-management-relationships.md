# Story 9.2.3: Supplier Management & Relationships

**Epic**: [epic-09-inventory-management.md](../epics/epic-09-inventory-management.md)
**Status**: not_started
**Domain**: Both

## User Story

_As a procurement manager, I want comprehensive supplier management so that I can maintain reliable sourcing and optimize purchasing decisions._

## Acceptance Criteria

- (NOT BUILT) Complete supplier database with contact information, terms, performance metrics, and history
- (NOT BUILT) Supplier performance tracking including on-time delivery, quality scores, and price competitiveness
- (NOT BUILT) Multi-supplier sourcing with automatic price comparison and availability checking
- (NOT BUILT) Supplier communication tools with order status tracking and issue resolution
- (NOT BUILT) Contract management with terms, pricing tiers, and automatic renewal notifications
- (NOT BUILT) Supplier diversity tracking and reporting for business development purposes
- (NOT BUILT) Credit and payment term management with automatic payment scheduling
- (NOT BUILT) Supplier risk assessment including financial stability and market position analysis
- (NOT BUILT) Exclusive product access tracking for limited releases and special editions
- (NOT BUILT) Integration with supplier catalogs and real-time inventory feeds

## Implementation Notes

Supplier management requires a comprehensive database with contact information, performance metrics, and contract management. Multi-supplier sourcing with price comparison and risk assessment enables optimized purchasing decisions. Integration with supplier catalogs provides real-time availability for automated procurement workflows.

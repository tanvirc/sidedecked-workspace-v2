# Story 10.5.1: Comprehensive Financial Reporting

**Epic**: [epic-10-payment-processing.md](../epics/epic-10-payment-processing.md)
**Status**: not_started
**Domain**: Commerce (backend/)

## User Story

_As a business operator, I want detailed financial reporting so that I can understand payment performance and make informed business decisions._

## Acceptance Criteria

- (NOT BUILT) Real-time payment analytics with transaction volume, value, and success rate metrics
- (NOT BUILT) Customizable reporting periods with year-over-year comparisons and trend analysis
- (NOT BUILT) Commission and fee reporting with breakdown by seller, category, and time period
- (NOT BUILT) Chargeback and dispute reporting with root cause analysis and prevention insights
- (NOT BUILT) Currency performance reporting with exchange rate impact and hedging recommendations
- (NOT BUILT) Seller performance analytics including payment velocity and dispute rates
- (NOT BUILT) Tax reporting integration with automatic categorization and jurisdiction breakdown
- (NOT BUILT) Cash flow forecasting with pending transaction impact and payout scheduling
- (NOT BUILT) Integration with business intelligence tools for advanced analytics and visualization
- (NOT BUILT) Automated report generation and delivery for stakeholders and regulatory requirements

## Implementation Notes

Comprehensive financial reporting delivers real-time payment analytics with customizable periods and year-over-year comparisons. Commission and fee reporting, chargeback analysis, and currency performance tracking provide operational insights. Cash flow forecasting, BI tool integration, and automated stakeholder report delivery complete the reporting system.

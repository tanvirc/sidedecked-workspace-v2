# Story 4.3.2: Market Intelligence Dashboard

**Epic**: [epic-04-vendor-management.md](../epics/epic-04-vendor-management.md)
**Status**: not_started
**Domain**: Both

## User Story

_As a vendor, I want market intelligence so that I can make informed decisions about inventory acquisition and pricing._

## Acceptance Criteria

- (NOT BUILT) Market trend analysis for different card categories and individual high-value cards
- (NOT BUILT) Demand forecasting based on tournament results, format changes, and seasonal patterns
- (NOT BUILT) Price movement alerts for cards in vendor inventory
- (NOT BUILT) Acquisition opportunity identification showing underpriced cards in the market
- (NOT BUILT) Format impact analysis predicting price changes from rotation and ban list updates
- (NOT BUILT) Supply/demand analysis for inventory planning and acquisition decisions
- (NOT BUILT) Competitor analysis showing market positioning and pricing strategies
- (NOT BUILT) Investment recommendations for cards with strong growth potential
- (NOT BUILT) Market volatility indicators helping vendors manage risk
- (NOT BUILT) Custom watchlists for tracking specific cards or market segments

## Implementation Notes

The market intelligence dashboard would be located at `/vendor/intelligence`. Components would include MarketTrends (trend visualization), DemandForecasting (predictive analytics), AcquisitionOpportunities (underpriced card highlighting), FormatImpact (meta change tracking), and CustomWatchlists. Multi-channel alerts via email, SMS, push notifications, and in-app messaging would be configurable.

# Story 9.3.3: Performance Benchmarking & KPIs

**Epic**: [epic-09-inventory-management.md](../epics/epic-09-inventory-management.md)
**Status**: not_started
**Domain**: Both

## User Story

_As a vendor, I want inventory performance benchmarking so that I can compare my performance against industry standards and identify improvement opportunities._

## Acceptance Criteria

- (NOT BUILT) Industry benchmark comparison for turnover rates, holding costs, and profitability
- (NOT BUILT) Peer group analysis comparing similar-sized vendors and market participants
- (NOT BUILT) Goal setting and tracking for key inventory metrics with progress monitoring
- (NOT BUILT) Performance alerts for metrics falling outside acceptable ranges or targets
- (NOT BUILT) Custom KPI dashboard with real-time updates and historical trend analysis
- (NOT BUILT) Inventory efficiency scoring with recommendations for improvement areas
- (NOT BUILT) Best practice recommendations based on top-performing similar vendors
- (NOT BUILT) Cost structure analysis comparing inventory costs to revenue and profit margins
- (NOT BUILT) Market opportunity identification based on underperforming inventory categories
- (NOT BUILT) Predictive analytics for future performance based on current trends and market conditions

## Implementation Notes

Performance benchmarking compares vendor metrics against industry standards and peer groups. Custom KPI dashboards with real-time updates, efficiency scoring, and best practice recommendations provide actionable improvement guidance. Predictive analytics for future performance based on current trends completes the benchmarking system.

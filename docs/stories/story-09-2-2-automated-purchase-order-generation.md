# Story 9.2.2: Automated Purchase Order Generation

**Epic**: [epic-09-inventory-management.md](../epics/epic-09-inventory-management.md)
**Status**: not_started
**Domain**: Both

## User Story

_As a buyer, I want automated purchase order generation so that I can efficiently restock inventory without manual monitoring._

## Acceptance Criteria

- (NOT BUILT) Customizable reorder points based on sales velocity, lead times, and safety stock levels
- (NOT BUILT) Automated supplier selection based on cost, reliability, shipping times, and terms
- (NOT BUILT) Purchase order optimization considering volume discounts, shipping costs, and cash flow
- (NOT BUILT) Budget constraint integration with spending limits and cash flow management
- (NOT BUILT) Seasonal adjustment for reorder quantities based on historical demand patterns
- (NOT BUILT) Supplier integration with API-based ordering for major distributors and wholesalers
- (NOT BUILT) Approval workflows for high-value or unusual purchase orders
- (NOT BUILT) Purchase order tracking with delivery confirmation and inventory receipt automation
- (NOT BUILT) Cost variance analysis comparing actual purchase costs to budgeted amounts
- (NOT BUILT) Integration with accounting systems for automatic expense recording and payment processing

## Implementation Notes

Automated purchase order generation uses configurable reorder points and supplier selection logic. Budget constraints, seasonal adjustments, and approval workflows are required for safe automated procurement. Accounting system integration enables seamless expense recording for all generated orders.

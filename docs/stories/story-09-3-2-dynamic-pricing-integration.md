# Story 9.3.2: Dynamic Pricing Integration

**Epic**: [epic-09-inventory-management.md](../epics/epic-09-inventory-management.md)
**Status**: not_started
**Domain**: Both

## User Story

_As a seller, I want inventory management integrated with dynamic pricing so that I can optimize both stock levels and profit margins._

## Acceptance Criteria

- (NOT BUILT) Real-time price adjustment based on inventory levels and market conditions
- (NOT BUILT) Clearance pricing automation for slow-moving and aging inventory
- (NOT BUILT) Dynamic pricing for high-velocity items based on competition and demand
- (NOT BUILT) Inventory-driven promotion generation for overstocked items
- (NOT BUILT) Market condition response with automatic repricing during volatile periods
- (NOT BUILT) Customer segment pricing with inventory allocation for different buyer types
- (NOT BUILT) Bundle creation recommendations for moving slow inventory with popular items
- (NOT BUILT) Price elasticity analysis to optimize pricing strategies for inventory movement
- (NOT BUILT) Profit margin protection with minimum price floors based on cost basis
- (NOT BUILT) Integration with external pricing intelligence and market data sources

## Implementation Notes

Dynamic pricing integration connects inventory levels with real-time price adjustments and clearance automation. Bundle creation recommendations, customer segment pricing, and price elasticity analysis optimize inventory movement. Minimum price floors based on cost basis protect profit margins while enabling aggressive clearance pricing for aged stock.

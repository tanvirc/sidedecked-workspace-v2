# Story 10.3.3: Cross-Border Tax Management

**Epic**: [epic-10-payment-processing.md](../epics/epic-10-payment-processing.md)
**Status**: not_started
**Domain**: Commerce (backend/)

## User Story

_As an international seller, I want automatic tax calculation and reporting so that I can comply with international tax obligations without complexity._

## Acceptance Criteria

- (NOT BUILT) Automatic VAT/GST calculation based on buyer and seller locations
- (NOT BUILT) Tax threshold monitoring with automatic registration recommendations for sellers
- (NOT BUILT) Integration with tax service providers for complex international tax scenarios
- (NOT BUILT) Digital goods tax compliance for digital card images and online-only products
- (NOT BUILT) Import duty calculation and disclosure for international shipments
- (NOT BUILT) Tax-inclusive and tax-exclusive pricing options based on local market standards
- (NOT BUILT) Automatic tax invoice generation with required local formatting and information
- (NOT BUILT) Year-end tax reporting with comprehensive transaction summaries and categorization
- (NOT BUILT) Integration with accounting systems for seamless tax filing preparation
- (NOT BUILT) Expert tax advisory service integration for complex international transactions

## Implementation Notes

Cross-border tax management automatically calculates VAT/GST based on buyer and seller locations with tax threshold monitoring for registration recommendations. Digital goods tax, import duty calculation, and automatic invoice generation with local formatting meet international compliance requirements. Year-end reporting integrates with accounting systems for seamless tax filing.

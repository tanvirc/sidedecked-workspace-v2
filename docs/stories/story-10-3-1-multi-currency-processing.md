# Story 10.3.1: Multi-Currency Processing

**Epic**: [epic-10-payment-processing.md](../epics/epic-10-payment-processing.md)
**Status**: not_started
**Domain**: Commerce (backend/)

## User Story

_As an international seller, I want to accept payments in multiple currencies so that I can serve global customers without currency barriers._

## Acceptance Criteria

- (NOT BUILT) Support for 25+ major currencies with real-time exchange rate integration
- (NOT BUILT) Automatic currency conversion at point of sale with transparent fee disclosure
- (NOT BUILT) Seller payout in preferred currency with competitive exchange rates
- (NOT BUILT) Currency hedging options for sellers with significant international exposure
- (NOT BUILT) Regional payment method support (SEPA, iDEAL, Alipay, etc.) based on buyer location
- (NOT BUILT) Tax calculation integration for international transactions with VAT/GST compliance
- (NOT BUILT) Multi-currency reporting and analytics for sellers and marketplace operators
- (NOT BUILT) Dynamic pricing display in buyer's local currency with conversion transparency
- (NOT BUILT) Cross-border transaction compliance with international banking regulations
- (NOT BUILT) Integration with local banking systems for faster settlement in supported regions

## Implementation Notes

Multi-currency processing supports 25+ currencies with real-time exchange rates and transparent conversion fees. Regional payment methods including SEPA, iDEAL, and Alipay are activated based on buyer location. Currency hedging, VAT/GST compliance, and local banking integrations enable full international payment support.

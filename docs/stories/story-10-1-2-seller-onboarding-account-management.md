# Story 10.1.2: Seller Onboarding & Account Management

**Epic**: [epic-10-payment-processing.md](../epics/epic-10-payment-processing.md)
**Status**: in_progress
**Domain**: Commerce (backend/)

## User Story

_As a seller, I want streamlined payment account setup so that I can start receiving payments quickly and manage my financial information easily._

## Acceptance Criteria

- (IMPLEMENTED) Simplified seller onboarding with progressive disclosure of required information
- (IMPLEMENTED) Real-time validation of bank account information and tax documentation
- (IMPLEMENTED) Support for individual and business account types with appropriate documentation requirements
- (IN PROGRESS) International seller support with local bank account and tax compliance (PARTIAL)
- (IMPLEMENTED) Account verification status tracking with clear next steps and requirements
- (IMPLEMENTED) Seller dashboard for payment history, pending payouts, and account settings
- (IMPLEMENTED) Tax form generation and delivery (1099-K in US, appropriate forms internationally)
- (IMPLEMENTED) Account suspension and reactivation procedures for compliance or fraud issues
- (IN PROGRESS) Multi-user access for business accounts with role-based permissions (PARTIAL)
- (IN PROGRESS) Integration with existing business banking relationships where possible (PARTIAL)

## Implementation Notes

Seller onboarding uses a step-by-step progressive flow with real-time validation for both individual and business account types. Account verification status tracking and payout management are implemented. International compliance, multi-user role-based permissions, and deeper banking relationship integration are partially built and need completion.

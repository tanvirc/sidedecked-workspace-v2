# Story 10.2.2: Authentication & Verification Services

**Epic**: [epic-10-payment-processing.md](../epics/epic-10-payment-processing.md)
**Status**: not_started
**Domain**: Commerce (backend/)

## User Story

_As a trader of valuable cards, I want authentication services integrated with payments so that I can ensure item authenticity before completing transactions._

## Acceptance Criteria

- (NOT BUILT) Integration with professional grading services (PSA, BGS, SGC) for authentication
- (NOT BUILT) Photo-based condition verification with expert review for disputed items
- (NOT BUILT) Tamper-evident packaging requirements with void indicators for high-value items
- (NOT BUILT) Chain of custody tracking from seller through authentication to buyer delivery
- (NOT BUILT) Expert authentication network for specialized or unusual items
- (NOT BUILT) Digital certification and blockchain provenance tracking for ultra-high-value items
- (NOT BUILT) Insurance claim integration for items damaged during authentication process
- (NOT BUILT) Seller certification programs for trusted high-volume sellers
- (NOT BUILT) Buyer education resources about authentication processes and timelines
- (NOT BUILT) Integration with fraud detection systems for suspicious transaction patterns

## Implementation Notes

Authentication and verification integrates with PSA, BGS, and SGC grading services for professional card authentication. Chain of custody tracking, blockchain provenance, and tamper-evident packaging requirements protect high-value transactions. Seller certification programs and buyer education resources build marketplace trust.

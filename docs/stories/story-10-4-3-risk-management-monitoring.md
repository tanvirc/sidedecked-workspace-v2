# Story 10.4.3: Risk Management & Monitoring

**Epic**: [epic-10-payment-processing.md](../epics/epic-10-payment-processing.md)
**Status**: not_started
**Domain**: Commerce (backend/)

## User Story

_As a risk manager, I want comprehensive risk monitoring so that I can identify and mitigate potential financial and operational risks._

## Acceptance Criteria

- (NOT BUILT) Real-time transaction monitoring with customizable risk rules and thresholds
- (NOT BUILT) Comprehensive dashboards for risk metrics, trends, and anomaly detection
- (NOT BUILT) Automated risk scoring for sellers based on transaction patterns and dispute history
- (NOT BUILT) Integration with credit bureaus and business verification services
- (NOT BUILT) Chargeback prediction and prevention with proactive dispute management
- (NOT BUILT) Reserve fund management for high-risk sellers with automatic adjustment
- (NOT BUILT) Risk-based authentication with step-up verification for suspicious transactions
- (NOT BUILT) Integration with external threat intelligence feeds for emerging fraud patterns
- (NOT BUILT) Regulatory risk monitoring with compliance alert systems
- (NOT BUILT) Financial risk assessment including credit risk and settlement risk management

## Implementation Notes

Risk management uses real-time transaction monitoring with customizable rules and automated seller risk scoring. Reserve fund management adjusts automatically for high-risk sellers. Chargeback prediction, credit bureau integration, and external threat intelligence feeds provide proactive risk mitigation. Step-up authentication activates for suspicious transaction patterns.

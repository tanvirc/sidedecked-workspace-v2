# Story 8.5.2: Community-Generated Content Discovery

**Epic**: [epic-08-search-discovery.md](../epics/epic-08-search-discovery.md)
**Status**: not_started
**Domain**: Both

## User Story

_As a user, I want to discover high-quality community content so that I can learn from others and engage with valuable discussions and resources._

## Acceptance Criteria

- (NOT BUILT) Curated content discovery featuring high-quality guides, deck techs, and analyses
- (NOT BUILT) Community-voted content rankings for quality surfacing
- (NOT BUILT) Content categorization by type (strategy, trading, collection, events)
- (NOT BUILT) Expert-authored content highlighting with credibility indicators
- (NOT BUILT) Fresh content discovery for newly published high-quality posts
- (NOT BUILT) Personalized content feed based on game preferences and follow list
- (NOT BUILT) Trending discussions highlighting active community conversations
- (NOT BUILT) Content search across guides, forum posts, and community resources
- (NOT BUILT) Saved content library for bookmarked articles and discussions
- (NOT BUILT) Content recommendation based on recent searches and browsing history

## Implementation Notes

Community content discovery would curate the best user-generated content from forums, guides, and deck publications. Quality signals include upvotes, comments, saves, and view-to-engagement ratios. Expert credibility would be indicated by verified player/creator badges and reputation scores. Personalized content feeds would prioritize content from followed users and topics matching the user's active game interests and collection profile.

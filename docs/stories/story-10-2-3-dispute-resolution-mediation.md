# Story 10.2.3: Dispute Resolution & Mediation

**Epic**: [epic-10-payment-processing.md](../epics/epic-10-payment-processing.md)
**Status**: not_started
**Domain**: Commerce (backend/)

## User Story

_As a marketplace participant, I want fair dispute resolution so that I can resolve transaction issues without losing money or reputation._

## Acceptance Criteria

- (NOT BUILT) Structured dispute escalation process from direct negotiation to professional mediation
- (NOT BUILT) Evidence collection system with photo uploads, communication logs, and expert opinions
- (NOT BUILT) Time-bound resolution process with automatic escalation and decision deadlines
- (NOT BUILT) Professional mediation services for complex or high-value disputes
- (NOT BUILT) Partial refund capabilities for condition disputes with itemized resolution options
- (NOT BUILT) Seller protection against fraudulent claims with reputation system integration
- (NOT BUILT) Appeals process for disputed resolutions with independent review panels
- (NOT BUILT) Integration with legal services for disputes exceeding internal resolution capabilities
- (NOT BUILT) Comprehensive record keeping for legal compliance and pattern analysis
- (NOT BUILT) Machine learning integration for dispute prediction and prevention

## Implementation Notes

Dispute resolution uses a structured escalation process from direct negotiation through professional mediation to legal services where required. Evidence collection, time-bound deadlines, and partial refund capabilities ensure fair outcomes. Machine learning integration for dispute prediction and prevention reduces future incidents.

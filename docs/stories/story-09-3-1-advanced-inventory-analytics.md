# Story 9.3.1: Advanced Inventory Analytics

**Epic**: [epic-09-inventory-management.md](../epics/epic-09-inventory-management.md)
**Status**: not_started
**Domain**: Both

## User Story

_As a business owner, I want comprehensive inventory analytics so that I can make data-driven decisions about my inventory investment and management._

## Acceptance Criteria

- (NOT BUILT) Inventory turnover analysis by card, set, game, and category with trend identification
- (NOT BUILT) Dead stock identification with aging reports and liquidation recommendations
- (NOT BUILT) ABC analysis categorizing inventory by sales volume and profitability
- (NOT BUILT) Seasonal demand pattern analysis with stocking recommendations by time period
- (NOT BUILT) Profit margin analysis by inventory category with optimization suggestions
- (NOT BUILT) Cash flow impact analysis showing inventory investment versus return patterns
- (NOT BUILT) Space utilization optimization for physical storage and warehouse management
- (NOT BUILT) Carrying cost analysis including storage, insurance, and opportunity costs
- (NOT BUILT) Market share analysis within specific card categories and competitive positioning
- (NOT BUILT) ROI analysis for inventory investments with performance benchmarking

## Implementation Notes

Advanced inventory analytics includes turnover analysis, dead stock identification, ABC categorization, and ROI tracking. Seasonal demand patterns, carrying cost analysis, and space utilization optimization provide actionable guidance for inventory investment decisions. Market share and competitive positioning analysis complete the analytical picture.

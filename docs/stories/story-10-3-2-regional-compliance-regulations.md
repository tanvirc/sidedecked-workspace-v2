# Story 10.3.2: Regional Compliance & Regulations

**Epic**: [epic-10-payment-processing.md](../epics/epic-10-payment-processing.md)
**Status**: not_started
**Domain**: Commerce (backend/)

## User Story

_As a global marketplace, I want comprehensive regulatory compliance so that I can operate legally in all supported jurisdictions._

## Acceptance Criteria

- (NOT BUILT) Know Your Customer (KYC) compliance with regional identity verification requirements
- (NOT BUILT) Anti-Money Laundering (AML) monitoring with transaction pattern analysis and reporting
- (NOT BUILT) Tax reporting compliance for all supported jurisdictions with automatic form generation
- (NOT BUILT) Consumer protection compliance with local refund and dispute resolution laws
- (NOT BUILT) Data privacy compliance (GDPR, CCPA) for payment and personal information
- (NOT BUILT) Banking regulation compliance for money transmission and marketplace operations
- (NOT BUILT) Regional blocked lists and sanctions screening for all transactions
- (NOT BUILT) Local consumer rights integration with automatic policy adjustment
- (NOT BUILT) Regulatory reporting automation for government compliance requirements
- (NOT BUILT) Legal framework integration for contract enforcement and dispute resolution

## Implementation Notes

Regional compliance covers KYC identity verification, AML transaction monitoring, and sanctions screening for all transactions. GDPR and CCPA data privacy requirements apply to all payment data. Automated regulatory reporting and jurisdiction-specific consumer protection rules ensure legal operation across all supported markets.

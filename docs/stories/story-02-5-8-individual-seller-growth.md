# Story 2.5.8: Individual Seller Growth & Success

**Epic**: [epic-02-commerce-marketplace.md](../epics/epic-02-commerce-marketplace.md)
**Status**: deferred
**Domain**: Commerce (backend/)

## User Story

_As an individual seller, I want resources and tools to improve my selling success so that I can maximize my revenue and customer satisfaction._

## Acceptance Criteria

- (NOT BUILT) Seller education resources tailored to individual collectors
- (NOT BUILT) Best practices guide for photographing cards and writing descriptions
- (NOT BUILT) Pricing strategy advice based on market data and competition
- (NOT BUILT) Performance analytics dashboard with actionable insights
- (NOT BUILT) Seasonal selling tips and market trend information
- (NOT BUILT) Success stories and case studies from other individual sellers
- (NOT BUILT) Community forum access for seller peer support and advice
- (NOT BUILT) Optional seller training modules with completion certificates
- (NOT BUILT) Performance benchmarking against similar individual sellers
- (NOT BUILT) Goal setting and progress tracking tools
- (NOT BUILT) Integration with pricing intelligence for competitive insights
- (NOT BUILT) Mobile-accessible learning resources and tips
- (NOT BUILT) Notification system for new educational content and opportunities
- (NOT BUILT) Seller level progression rewards and recognition system
- (NOT BUILT) Access to seller-only features and community events

## Implementation Notes

The seller education hub would be at `/sell/resources` with performance insights at `/sell/analytics`. Resource categories cover Photography Tips, Pricing Strategies, Customer Service, and Shipping & Handling. The PerformanceAnalytics component shows listing views, conversion rates, average sale price trends, and buyer satisfaction scores. Benchmarking is against similar anonymous sellers for competitive context without exposing individual data.

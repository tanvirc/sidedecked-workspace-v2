# Story 7.4.2: Educational Content & Market Commentary

**Epic**: [epic-07-pricing-intelligence.md](../epics/epic-07-pricing-intelligence.md)
**Status**: not_started
**Domain**: Customer Experience (customer-backend/)

## User Story

_As a new collector, I want educational content and expert commentary so that I can learn about the market and improve my collecting knowledge._

## Acceptance Criteria

- (NOT BUILT) Beginner's guide to TCG card collecting and valuation
- (NOT BUILT) Market terminology glossary with plain-language explanations
- (NOT BUILT) Weekly market commentary from experienced collectors and traders
- (NOT BUILT) Video content library covering valuation methodology and market analysis
- (NOT BUILT) Interactive learning modules with quizzes and practice exercises
- (NOT BUILT) Market news integration from major TCG news sources
- (NOT BUILT) Expert analysis of major market events and their implications
- (NOT BUILT) Community-contributed guides with quality moderation
- (NOT BUILT) Learning path progression from beginner to advanced market understanding
- (NOT BUILT) Personalized content recommendations based on collection and experience level

## Implementation Notes

Educational content would be organized by skill level and TCG game. Weekly market commentary would be authored by platform-vetted experts and experienced community members. News integration would aggregate content from major TCG news sources with editorial curation. Interactive modules would include practical exercises like identifying card conditions, comparing prices, and evaluating trade fairness. Learning paths would guide new collectors through the essential knowledge for successful collecting.

# Story 4.6.2: Shipping Management & Optimization

**Epic**: [epic-04-vendor-management.md](../epics/epic-04-vendor-management.md)
**Status**: not_started
**Domain**: Both

## User Story

_As a vendor, I want advanced shipping tools so that I can reduce shipping costs and improve delivery times._

## Acceptance Criteria

- (NOT BUILT) Multi-carrier rate comparison for optimal shipping choices
- (NOT BUILT) Shipping rule automation based on order value, destination, and item characteristics
- (NOT BUILT) Shipping cost optimization with zone skipping and consolidation suggestions
- (NOT BUILT) International shipping support with customs documentation
- (NOT BUILT) Shipping insurance options and damage claim management
- (NOT BUILT) Delivery performance tracking and carrier performance analysis
- (NOT BUILT) Shipping cost analysis and profitability reporting
- (NOT BUILT) Customer shipping preference management and communication
- (NOT BUILT) Exception handling for failed deliveries and address issues

## Implementation Notes

The shipping optimization dashboard would be located at `/vendor/shipping`. It would feature real-time rate comparison across USPS, UPS, FedEx, and DHL, with a visual shipping rules engine for condition-based routing. Cost optimization tools would include zone skipping analysis and package optimization suggestions. International shipping support would cover customs documentation automation and duty/tax calculation.

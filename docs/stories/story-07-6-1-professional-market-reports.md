# Story 7.6.1: Professional Market Reports

**Epic**: [epic-07-pricing-intelligence.md](../epics/epic-07-pricing-intelligence.md)
**Status**: not_started
**Domain**: Customer Experience (customer-backend/)

## User Story

_As a business owner, I want professional-grade market reports so that I can make strategic business decisions and track industry trends._

## Acceptance Criteria

- (NOT BUILT) Weekly and monthly market summary reports with key metrics and trends
- (NOT BUILT) Game-specific market reports covering MTG, Pokemon, Yu-Gi-Oh!, and One Piece
- (NOT BUILT) Set release impact reports analyzing market reactions to new product
- (NOT BUILT) Format rotation reports modeling price changes from rule updates
- (NOT BUILT) Vendor performance reports for marketplace participants
- (NOT BUILT) Custom report builder with flexible metric and visualization selection
- (NOT BUILT) Report scheduling with automatic delivery to email or dashboard
- (NOT BUILT) Report sharing and distribution to team members
- (NOT BUILT) Report archiving with searchable history
- (NOT BUILT) White-label report generation for business use

## Implementation Notes

Market reports would be generated from the underlying data warehouse with pre-built templates for common report types. The custom report builder would use a drag-and-drop interface for adding metrics, charts, and tables. Reports would be available in PDF, Excel, and interactive web formats. Scheduled delivery would support daily, weekly, and monthly cadences. White-label options would allow vendors to brand reports with their own logo for sharing with stakeholders.

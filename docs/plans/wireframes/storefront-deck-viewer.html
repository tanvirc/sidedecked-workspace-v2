I've been running a similar list but with 2x Shard Volley instead of Chain Lightning. Have you tested that swap? Chain Lightning feels slow in the mirror match when they can pay {R}{R} to copy it back.
♥ 12 Reply
@pyromancer99 Author 2 hours ago
Good point about the mirror! In my testing, Chain Lightning is better in ~80% of matchups because the copy clause rarely matters outside burn mirrors. Shard Volley's land sac cost is too steep when you're already on 18 lands.
♥ 8 Reply
@spikeTCG 1 day ago
Took this exact 75 to FNM last Friday and went 4-0. The sideboard Leyline of Punishment is incredible against the Lifegain decks running rampant in my meta. Only change I'd suggest is finding room for a 3rd Exquisite Firecraft for the control matchup.
♥ 24 Reply
@budgetbrewer 2 days ago
Love that this is labeled Budget-Friendly. Under $130 for a competitive Modern deck is a steal. For anyone building on a tighter budget, you can replace Goblin Guide with Kumano Faces Kakkazan and save ~$40 with only a small performance hit.

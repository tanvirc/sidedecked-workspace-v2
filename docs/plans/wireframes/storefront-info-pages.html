Our Mission

SideDecked was founded by competitive TCG players who were frustrated with existing marketplaces. We believe buying and selling cards should be as exciting as playing them.

Our mission is to create the most trusted, player-focused marketplace for trading card games, with fair prices, verified conditions, and a community-driven experience.

What Makes Us Different

Unlike generic marketplaces, SideDecked is built specifically for the TCG community. Our platform includes card-specific data enrichment, condition verification systems, and seller reputation tracking designed for card traders.

We support Magic: The Gathering, Pokémon, Yu-Gi-Oh!, and One Piece with deep game-specific integrations including deck building, price tracking, and community features.

Our Team

We are a team of competitive players, developers, and entrepreneurs united by our passion for trading card games. Based in Australia, serving the global TCG community.

Frequently Asked Questions
Find answers to common questions about buying, selling, and trading on SideDecked.
About FAQ Terms of Service Privacy Policy Contact Seller Terms
How do I list a card for sale? ▼
To list a card, navigate to the Sell section and click "List a Card." You can search for any card by name, set it to the correct condition, add photos, and set your price. Our system will suggest a price based on current market data.
What payment methods are accepted? ▼
We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers through our secure payment partner Stripe. All transactions are protected by our buyer protection program.
How does buyer protection work? ▼
Every purchase on SideDecked is covered by our buyer protection guarantee. If a card arrives in a different condition than described, or doesn't arrive at all, you can file a claim within 14 days.
What games does SideDecked support? ▼
We currently support Magic: The Gathering, Pokémon TCG, Yu-Gi-Oh!, and One Piece Card Game, with more games planned.
How are shipping costs calculated? ▼
Shipping costs depend on the seller's location, your delivery address, and the shipping method. Sellers set their own shipping rates.
Can I return a card after purchase? ▼
Returns are accepted within 14 days if the card condition doesn't match the listing. Contact the seller first, then open a return request if needed.

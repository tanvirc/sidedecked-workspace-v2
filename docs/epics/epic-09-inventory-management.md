# Epic 9: Inventory Management

**Status**: not_started
**Domain**: Both

## Goal

The Inventory Management System provides comprehensive multi-channel inventory tracking, automated reordering, procurement management, and warehouse integration for vendors of all sizes. It delivers intelligent demand forecasting, dynamic pricing integration, and ERP connectivity to help vendors optimize their card inventory investment and fulfillment operations across multiple sales channels.

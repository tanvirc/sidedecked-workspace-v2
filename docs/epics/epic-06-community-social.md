# Epic 6: Community & Social

**Status**: not_started
**Domain**: Customer Experience (customer-backend/)

## Goal

The Community & Social System enables users to connect, communicate, and build relationships within the SideDecked platform. It provides comprehensive social networking features including direct messaging, group communities, forums, trading, and local event organization. The system fosters a vibrant TCG community by enabling users to share knowledge, discuss strategies, organize trades, and meet fellow collectors and players.

# Epic 7: Pricing Intelligence

**Status**: not_started
**Domain**: Customer Experience (customer-backend/)

## Goal

The Pricing Intelligence System provides comprehensive market data aggregation, advanced analytics, and real-time price monitoring for all trading cards on the platform. It delivers machine learning-powered price predictions, portfolio tracking, and market research tools to help collectors, investors, and vendors make informed decisions about card values and market trends across all supported TCG games.
